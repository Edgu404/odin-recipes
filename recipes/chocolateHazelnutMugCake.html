Chocolate Hazelnut Mug Cake

Description

A quick and easy mug cake that will bring the sense of comfort that only a warm dessert can.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup BOOST original powder-Chocolate
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 Tsp unsweetend cocoal powder
  • 2 Tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 2/3 cup 2% milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tsp hazelnut chocolate spread
  • 2 tsp chopped hazelnuts

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl combineBOOST original Powder, flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder and salt.
  2. Whisk in milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla until smooth and well-combined.
  3. Pour batter into microwave-safe mugs, no more than 3/4 full. Add 1tsp of hazelnut chocolate spread into the middle of each cup. Top with hazelnuts.
  4. Microwave mugs on high for 60-90 seconds, or until batter looks firm.
