Grilled Chesse Sandwich

Description

The grilled ham and cheese sandwich is a classic american comfort food. Warm slices of deli ham topped with melted cheddar cheese nested between two slices of perfectly toasted buttered white bread.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of honey-glazed ham
  • Two slices of orange cheeddar cheese
  • Two slices fresh white american bread
  • 1 Tbs of unsalted butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a thick bottomed frying pan on medium low heat(about 3 minutes).
  2. Butter one side of each of the pieces of bread.
  3. Place one pice of the bread, butter side down, into the pan.
  4. layer the ham and the cheese onto the bread. Top with the other piece of bread.
  5. When the bottom piece of bread becomes golden brown flip it.
  6. Like wise toast the other side of the sandwich.
  7. Once both sides are toasted to perfection remove the sandwich from the pan.
  8. Serve the sandwich with a bowl of your favorite soup.
