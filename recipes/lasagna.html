Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian-American dish consisting of meat, cheese, pastas, and red sauce.
It is baked like a casserole.

Inredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 Tsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz Marinara sauce
  • 1 Tbs tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Lasanga Noodles/Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cupts mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bolw and set aside.
  2. Heat olve oil over medium heat and add the diced onion. Let them stoften for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and sausage to the pan. Increase the temperature to medium high breaking up the meat until it's fine and crumbled. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through. add the garlic during the last minute of cooking then drain any excess grease.
  4. Deglaze the pan using the chicken broth Add the marninara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring the mix to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let he sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees(F) and bring a lage pot of salted water to boil
  6. Cook the noodles al denta
  7. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process
  8. Lay the noodles on a piece of wax paper or a sil mat. Do not lay them on top of each other or they might stick together.
  9. In a 9x13-inch casserole dish spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce.
  10. Lay lasagna noodles next to each other to cover the meat sauce.(overlapping them as needed is ok)
  11. Spread 1/3 of hte ricotta mix over the noodles followed by about 1.5 cups of the meat sauce.
  12. Repeat layering with thenoodles, ricotta, and meat sauce two more times.
  13. Top with 2.5 cups of mozzarella cheese.
  14. Cover the casserole dish with a pice of aluminum foil sprayed with nonstick cooking spray.
  15. Bake for 35 minutes then remove the foil and take for another 10 minutes.
  16. Let the casserole cool for about 15 minutes before serving.
